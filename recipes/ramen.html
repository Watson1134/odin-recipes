Ramen

Description

This recipe is a simple way of making pre-packaged ramen. The really cheap stuff. Like 0.89 cents stuff

Ingredients

  • Packet of Ramen
  • Water
  • Chicken bouillon cube
  • Other toppings you may enjoy (carrots, egg, green onions)

Steps

  1. Place 2 cups of water into pot with 2 bouillon cubes and bring to a boil
  2. Add ramen and flavoring packet
  3. stir until noodles are soft
  4. serve in bowl and add toppings
  5. Enjoy!
