Ramen

Description

This recipe is a simple way of making pre-packaged ramen. The really cheap stuff. Like 0.89 cents

Ingredients

  • Packet of Ramen
  • Water
  • Chicken bouljion cube
  • Other toppings you may enjoy (carrots, egg, green onions)

Steps

  1. Place 3 chicken breast in crock pot
  2. Add 2 cups of soy sauce
  3. Add 1 cup of honey
  4. Add 1 cup of sriracha
  5. Let cook on low for 6 hours
  6. Add 2 cups of rice and shred chicken/stir until rice is cooked
  7. Enjoy!
